Humudity

There is not a strong correlation between humidity and latitude. Many people believe that there is a strong correlation between the two since warmer temperatures can be very humid. However, many warm climates such as deserts can also be quite arid. Humidity levels are likely due to factors such as proximity to large bodies of water and air pressure.
