Max Temperature

As expected, the wetaher becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg. Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of years than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth at the time of year this data was gathered.
